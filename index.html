  • ✔ Individuals with full-time jobs or demanding schedules

  • ✔ Professionals who feel it’s “too late” to start memorizing
  • ✔ Anyone seeking a structured and guided hafazan system

❌ Not suitable for those looking for instant results or zero commitment.
